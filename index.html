I am a mathematics research fellow at Wesleyan University working with Cameron Hill. Here is my CV.

I will be starting my PhD in mathematics at UC Berkeley in the fall. My current research interests include graph theory, combinatorics, and commutative algebra.

Outside of academics, I enjoy writing creative non-fiction and poetry. I also like traveling and exploring the world. I am a herbivore and an animal rights supporter.
