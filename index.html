I am a second-year PhD student in the mathematics department at UC Berkeley. Here is my CV. My current research interests include combinatorics, nonlinear algebra, and algebraic statistics.

I am grateful for the support of the NSF Graduate Research Fellowship and the UC Berkeley Chancellor's Fellowship.
